p5play and Asteroids

One of the options for the project in the course was to create a game. I thought about that, but decided on building a portfolio instead.

While building that portfolio website, and doing some research, I found p5play, although too late into the project to use it to make a game. It adds capabilities to p5 for 2d gaming, such as collision, physics, sprites, rotation, and many more.

And I found this asteroids clone on the p5.js site, which uses p5play. It doesn't do everythiung (like UFO's, collisions of the ship), but its a great example of how simple this can be.

Go to asteroids on p5js.org

I found that I can understand the code, although it's a little beyond my skills at this point to code, and that our final p5 exercise was not that far from this. Testament to how good this course was.
